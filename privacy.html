Privacy Policy for Users in India

This Privacy Policy explains how Miracle Nature Labs collects, uses, shares and protects your personal data in compliance with the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDP Act), Information Technology Act, 2000, and other applicable Indian laws.

1. Who We Are

Miracle Nature Labs is an Indian company offering wellness and hygiene products including pregnancy test kits, intimate washes, gummies, multivitamins, Shilajit, and supplements. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring your data is handled responsibly.

2. Information We Collect

  • Information You Provide: Name, email, phone number, shipping address, payment details, and any health-related preferences you share through quizzes or forms (e.g., cycle tracking, wellness goals).
  • Information We Automatically Collect: Device information, IP address, browser type, pages visited, and cookies.
  • Information from Third Parties: Data from payment gateways, logistics partners, or analytics tools (with your consent where required).

3. Categories of Personal Data

We collect demographic data, contact details, transaction data, technical data, and limited sensitive personal data (only health preferences from optional quizzes, processed only with explicit consent).

4. Consent & Purpose of Processing

We process your data only with your consent or as permitted by law. Purposes include:

  • Processing orders and deliveries
  • Personalising product recommendations and wellness tips
  • Sending order updates, newsletters, and promotional offers
  • Improving our website and services
  • Preventing fraud and ensuring security
  • Complying with legal obligations

5. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use strictly necessary, functional, and performance cookies. You can manage preferences through your browser settings. We do not use Do Not Track signals as they are not standardised in India.

6. Sharing of Personal Data

We share data only with trusted partners (payment processors, logistics companies, marketing platforms) under strict contracts. We do not sell your personal data to third parties. Cross-border transfers (if any) are done with adequate safeguards.

7. Data Security & Retention

We use industry-standard encryption and security measures. Data is retained only as long as necessary for the purpose or as required by law (typically 5–7 years for transaction records).

8. Your Rights under DPDP Act

You have the right to access, correct, erase, or withdraw consent for your data. To exercise these rights, email us at the address below. We will respond within 30 days.

9. Children’s Privacy

Our products and services are intended for adults 18+. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 18. If we become aware of such data, we delete it immediately.

10. Health & Wellness Disclaimer

Important: Any health-related information you provide (e.g., through quizzes) is used only to improve recommendations and is never shared for marketing without separate consent. Our products are wellness items, not medicines. They do not diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any disease. Always consult a doctor before use.
